Machine Learning 2026

Credit Card Fraud Detection & Prediction

Problem: Identify fraudulent transactions from highly imbalanced data where fraud cases represent less than 1% of all transactions.

Approach: Applied SMOTE for rebalancing, Isolation Forest for anomaly detection, and built an ensemble (Logistic Regression + Random Forest + Gradient Boosting) with threshold optimization.

Impact: Achieved optimal precision-recall balance, enabling detection of fraud patterns in time and transaction amount distributions.
